The Nefties Cup 2020

Who likes random chances to win something? Um, everybody!

This is a game of chance. It's based on bitcoin blockchain's hash.

There are prizes!

  • 1st place - A champion NFT and a real, physical, hold-your-hand book. A copy of Kicking the Hornet's Nest
  • 2nd place - an NFT
  • 3rd place - an NFt
  • Participation awards - so snowflakes don't cry
  • Dead last - a boobie prize NFT

Details and rules in the works, but the upshot is...

There are 10 entry NFTs. If you hold one, you're in. You have a 1-in-10 chance of winning. The winner will be determined by the bitcoin blockchain. The last numeral in the hash of BTC block #655,202 will be the winner. That block should occur on October 31, 2020. That date was chosen in celebration of the release of the Bitcoin Whitepaper on October 31, 2008. And, that's why I'm giving away the book with all of Satoshi's words!
