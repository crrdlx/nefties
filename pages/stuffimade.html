Welcome to fabulous Bitkenstan.

Bitkenstan is the webcomic that became the world's first NFTcomic about bitcoin, cryptocurrency, blockchain, and life.

Bitkenstan is a series with a running storyline with over 100 episodes. As an NFTcomic, each episode can actually be owned by anyone. Imagine owning an episode of your favorite TV series...that's what NFTs enable.

Get more details at bitkenstan.com or view all of the episodes and NFTs.
