Potential of NFTs

Where have we been? Where are NFTs going?

The potential of NFTs seems immense.

You may have heard of CryptoKitties from 2017. They gave non-fungible tokens a first jumpstart. 2020 has seen NFTS take off even more...you've likely heard of Garbage Pale Kids. They're now NFTs. You've likely heard of POGS. They are are now KOGS, as NFTs. And, there's more. The future shows much, much greater things for NFTs.
