Welcome to nefties.com

nefties.com is about NFTs. Whereas cryptocurrencies are over a decade old now, NFTs are infants on the verge of booming. If you're viewing this site, you've likely heard of them on Ethereum as CryptoKitties. But, they are growing onto so many other blockchains: NEO, WAX, EOS, FLOW, NEAR, and I'm sure there are many others. It is early and it is happening.

If you're asking, "What's an NFT, read this. If you know about NFTs, then you know. Either way...
